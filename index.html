About Me

I’m an american-born videogame programmer located in Italy. I’m an all round developer that knows all the ins and outs of the Unity Engine and is capable of making the best out of the tools.


I started getting interested in game development when I was just 12 years old, but I became so passionate about it that in 2014 I decided that I wanted to make this my career and I self-taught myself most of the things I know today. I’ve attended high school that gave me a diploma in computer science in which I’ve learned many different things that go from networking to website development with HTML, CSS, PHP and MySQL. During my scholarship, I’ve also learned to code in Java and C++.


In 2016 I founded a videogame dev team called Poison Dagger Software. This has helped me grow a lot both professionally and personally, allowing me to develop social skills thanks to the events I’ve been to where I've presented my team’s videogame Two Sides Of Hell.
